About Packet Pals

Packet Pals is an innovative gamified network exploration platform that combines the excitement of collecting and battling unique creatures with the intrigue of network discovery. Our mission is to create a dynamic ecosystem where technology enthusiasts and gamers alike can engage in meaningful and entertaining interactions.

Mission
